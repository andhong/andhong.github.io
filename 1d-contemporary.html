Overview

1stdibs is a luxury marketplace that seeks to bring the most beautiful objects to buyers all over the world. While 1stdibs has previously been focused on antiques, vintage objects, and fine art, the company recently has been adjusting its strategy to include contemporary designers and makers.

I was tasked with designing a landing page that would act as a product showcase and homepage for 1stdibs.com. I designed the entire Contemporary Design homepage experience from establishing requirements with stakeholders all the way through to overseeing development.
Adapting to Endlessly Shifting Content

Creating a page for diverse and shifting products.

1stdibs sells an eclectic range of items from sellers from all over the world. With items ranging fom small desktop goods to furniture, the homepage needed to be modular enough to be able to highlight different types of makers, materials, techniques, and various other categories that were established with the marketing team (who would ultimately be responsible for the page content).
Test and Refine

Experimenting and Optimizing

This landing page is a constant work in progress. With more usage and data, we are able to update and swap modules to optimize for easy navigation and driving traffic to target areas of the site.
