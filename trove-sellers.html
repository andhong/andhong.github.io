Trove: Sellers Platform

| User Research | UX/UI Design | Graphic Design | Copy Editing |

The Trove Sellers Platform enables anyone to curate and sell their own jewelry line - no inventory or jewelry experience required. Users are guided through an intuitive onboarding experience where they can select from a library of jewelry pieces, set prices, and edit the look and feel of their storefront. They are then free to market their jewelry collection on social media, via facebook ads, and to friends and family. Whereas most selling platforms require some crafting experience to actually create the physical objects and ship them, we sought to develop a selling solution that makes it such that logistics do not get in the way of peoples' entrepreneurial drive.

The main challenge of designing the platform was to move people through a multi-step process while maintaining a high completion rate. This was accomplished by giving users incremental feedback and sectioning out major decisions so as to make things feel easier and more fun.
