Overview

Trove is a customizable jewelry platform where users are able to discover, customize, and purchase their own unique jewelry pieces. The in-browser customization tools allows anyone to create unique pieces regardless of their experience level with design programs. Trove originally launched with only its own in-house jewelry designs, but has since collaborated with other brands and social media influencers to develop private jewelry

My Role

I co-founded Trove alongside Brian Park and Tim Growney in 2015. I was the sole designer of the brand, site, and UX from top to bottom along with continual testing, refinement, and new feature development. Being the only designer for the platform helped me to maintain a consistent user experience from initial awareness of the Trove brand all the way to receiving and wearing our custom jewelry.

The identity of the site is clean and minimal in order to allow the jewelry itself to be the center of attention. This also allows for a great deal of flexibility in allowing us to create personalized storefronts for influencers, affiliate marketers, and others who want to create their own brands within the Trove platform.

The aesthetic of the packaging transitioned from a minimalism to something more playful as we developed a better understanding of our audience.
