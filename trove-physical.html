Trove: Physical Product

| Print Design | Packaging Design | Jewelry Design |

In 2015 I co-founded Trove alongside Brian Park and Tim Growney. Trove is a platform where users can discover, customize, and purchase their own unique 3D-printed jewelry pieces. In addition to leading the design and branding for the digital components of the platform, I was responsible for the physical product including packaging, print design, and jewelry design.

The aesthetic of the packaging transitioned from a minimalism to something more playful as we developed a better understanding of our audience.
